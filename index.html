This demo uses instruments and utilities from pelimanni, reverb via Dattorro's Reverb, and a set of basic Web Audio compressors and filters to keep the volume and ultra/infrasounds in check.

The music is an adaptation of a part of Heaven's Tower by Naoshi Mizuta, from Final Fantasy XI Original Soundtrack.
